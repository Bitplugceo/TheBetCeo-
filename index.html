VCF Cleaner

Upload your VCF once, split multi-number contacts into separate contacts, remove duplicate numbers globally, then keep pasting numbers you want removed. The current cleaned VCF stays saved in this browser until you press Clear Saved Session.

Step 1 — Load original VCF
This version does not use a country-code input. It keeps the current cleaned VCF saved on this device/browser until you clear it.
Step 2 — Remove numbers you paste
Each time you tap Remove Pasted Numbers, the page removes those numbers from the currently saved VCF and saves the updated file again automatically.
