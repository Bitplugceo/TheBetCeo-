VCF Cleaner

Upload your VCF once, split multi-number contacts into separate contacts, remove duplicate numbers globally, then keep pasting numbers you want removed. The current cleaned file stays saved in this browser until you clear it.

Step 1 — Load original VCF
This does not delete other countries. It only helps match local numbers like 0803... to +234803...
Step 2 — Remove numbers you paste
Every time you paste numbers here and tap remove, the page deletes them from the currently saved cleaned file and replaces it with the newer version.
